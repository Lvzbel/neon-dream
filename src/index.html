About Me

How and Why I got started with computer programming.

How I got started with programming.

A few years ago I started getting my feet wet with programming, I was mostly curious about Cyber Security, but that world at the time it seemed very obfuscated and with no clear path.

I decided to start with computer programming more specifically Python and C++. I took a college course in Computer Science 101 and the language they were using in the course was C++, on the other hand, I also decided to start learning on my own, a course I took online form Microsoft was focusing on Python.

My CyberSecurity Journey

After a couple of months learning Python, At that point I had basic knowledge, I started to dabble in CyberSecurity and discovered there were many paths, due to a friend influence I decided to learn Malware Analysis. I really enjoy the aspect of investigating and playing a cat and mouse game.

I Discovered Web Development

I decided to give Web Development a try because that was the majority of the courses I would find online. I always thought it would be cool to be able to sit and create something about basic lines of code. It was clear that Web Development was for me, I learn everything on own with the help of Udemy, Udacity, and books.

However, the Web Development world is full of different packages and frameworks that make it really hard for someone to pick the exact path to learn and it also makes me feel like I will never be ready or learn everything, but with my current skills, I feel very confident and ready to start my career.
